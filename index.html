Our advantages

  • Knowledgeable tutors who get the best from every student
  • All staff hold Enhanced DBS checks for total peace of mind
  • A welcoming, secure space where students feel supported
  • Smaller groups mean more attention and faster progress
  • We focus on confidence as much as results, and it shows
  • Based in Small Heath, easy to reach across Birmingham

How tutoring works

  1. Book a session Choose your subject and preferred time slot online or by phone.
  2. Free assessment Your child joins a small group for a relaxed first session so we can understand their needs.
  3. Personalised plan We set clear goals and match them to the right group and tutor.
  4. Weekly sessions Regular one-hour lessons in Maths, English or Science with homework support.
  5. Progress updates Parents receive regular feedback so you can see improvement over time.
